MilitaryPrep Academy

APPLY

Prepares scholars for a military career after college

The Military Preparatory Academy grants students the opportunity to understand the many moving parts of the United States military and prepare them for the Reserves Officer Training Corps. The academy is held in the afternoons at the Edgewood Campus. Scholars in this academy will take one mathematics class, one english class, and one Military Foundations class, taught by Chris Bryson, Amanda Dempsey, and co-teachers Chief Philips and Sergeant Davis respectively. Students applying can expect to undergo various drill routines, attend competitions, and receive their own military uniforms.
