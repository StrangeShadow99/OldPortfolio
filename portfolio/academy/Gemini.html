Gemini Academy

APPLY

Focuses on preparing high-school seniors for the years to come.

The Gemini College Academy focuses on honing the skills required to succeed in college and in the world after high-school, whilst offering dual-credit classes through Thomas More. The academy is held in the mornings at the Edgewood Campus. Scholars in this academy will take one dual-credit mathematics class, two dual-credit english classes, at least one dual-credit history class, and a Senior Academic Seminar class taught by Amanda Dempsey. The dual-credit classes are taught by Thomas More professors. Students applying can expect to take a second ACT exam paid for by the academy, as well as learn the essential skills to succeed in college classes.

Our Teachers

Mrs. Dempsey has been in education for nine years with three of them being in the Academies, and she is most passionate about connecting writing to the real world and helping scholars plan for their futures. She holds a B.A. in English with a concentration in creative writing and an M.A. in English with a certificate in composition and rhetoric. She is currently pursuing an Ed.D. in Educational Leadership. Mrs. Dempsey is a Google Level 1 and 2 certified educator and is certified to teach AP English Language. When she is not at school or doing homework, she is spending time with her husband chasing around her their two sons, Ian and Crosby, watching The Office, reading about true crime, or cooking.
